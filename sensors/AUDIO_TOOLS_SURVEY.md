## ⭐ Recommended Starting Points

### To explore immediately (web, no install):
1. **https://generative.fm** — Just open it. Infinite ambient music.
2. **https://binauro.com** — Binaural beats with spatial positioning
3. **https://moodist.mvze.net** — Clean soundscape mixer

### To install for deeper exploration:
1. **Gnaural** — The OG binaural tool, very configurable
2. **Blanket** (if on Linux) — Beautiful ambient mixer

### For integration with our project:
- **binaural-generator** Python package could feed into our HR-reactive system
- **Generative.fm is open source** — we could fork/modify to respond to physiological data

## The Vision Connection

These tools show what's possible. But they're all **static** — you press play and it runs.

What we're building is **reactive**:
- Your HR influences the soundscape
- Your presence/attention shapes the audio
- The system responds to you, not just plays at you

The gap between "ambient playlist" and "physiologically-responsive sound environment" is the interesting space.
